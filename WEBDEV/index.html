About Us

At SLSU Fitness Progress Tracker, we empower students across Lucena to achieve their fitness goals through a structured, student-focused program. Our initiative, designed specifically for the Southern Luzon State University (SLSU) community, offers over 90 tailored exercises to suit all fitness levels, from beginners to advanced athletes.

Our mission is to foster a supportive environment where students can track their progress, stay motivated, and build healthy habits that last a lifetime. Whether you're into cardio, yoga, or strength training, our program provides personalized tools and resources to help you succeed.

Join our vibrant community today and take the first step toward a stronger, healthier you!
